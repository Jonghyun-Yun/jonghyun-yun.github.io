Jonghyun Yun

Biography

He completed his PhD in the Department of Statistics at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign under the guidance of Dr. Yuguo Chen. Prior to joining UTA, he was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences and a core faculty in the Border Biomedical Research Center at the University of Texas at El Paso, from 2015 to 2016. He was a postdoctoral researcher at UT Southwestern Medical Center under the guidance of Drs. Guanghua Xiao and Yang Xie from 2012 to 2015.
